Our mission

"At Flora's African Restaurant, our mission is to bring the heart and soul of African cuisine to India, offering a truly homemade experience. We take pride in preparing fresh, authentic dishes from various African regions, infused with the warmth and love of home cooking. Our goal is to share the rich culinary heritage of Africa, providing our customers with a unique and flavorful journey, whether they are reconnecting with familiar tastes or discovering new favorites. Meet our team

Flora and her daughter are the culinary artists behind every dish at Flora's African Restaurant. Flora brings decades of experience and cherished family recipes from Cameroon, her home country, ensuring that each meal is authentically delicious. She has had over 10 years of experience as a professional cook and over a lifetime as a mum from cooking for her children. Her daughter, Sarah, adds a modern twist to these traditional dishes, blending creativity with heritage. Together, they craft meals that are a true celebration of African cuisine.
